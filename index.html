ABOUT COMPETITION

The competition aims to find optimal and innovative engineering solutions for the day to day problems faced by under served communities and to enhance critical thinking among students.
It is an idea proposal event for betterment of community. Participants have to register on the registration link provided in this website and all proposals will be accepted online e -formats only.All updates will be given here!.

  AIM

The AIM of this competition is to find optimal and innovative engineering solutions for the day to day problems faced by underserved communities and to enhance critical thinking of students thus leading to welfare of humanity.

  WHO

Students from First year to Final year of Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli can participate in this event.

  HOW

Participants can register through the link provided below. Further updates regarding the competition will be available Here!.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

It is duty of each and every engineer to offer their efforts towards underserved communities. Such efforts raise important questions. Which kind of help is required by community? How do engineers listen to a community? If invited, how do engineers work with a community?

So to find optimised solutions for day to day life problems students are required to propose an idea for Community Development Projects from their perspective for this contest. Students should provide an appropriate title for their project. They should list out all the problems that they plan to solve using this project. They are supposed to explain roughly how the project will work, using appropriate block diagrams/use case diagrams. They can choose the topic from following domains.

ABOUT COMPETITION

The competition aims to find optimal and innovative engineering solutions for the day to day problems faced by under served communities and to enhance critical thinking among students.
It is an idea proposal event for betterment of community. Participants have to register on the registration link provided in this website and all proposals will be accepted online e -formats only.All updates will be given here!.


  AIM

The AIM of this competition is to find optimal and innovative engineering solutions for the day to day problems faced by underserved communities and to enhance critical thinking of students thus leading to welfare of humanity.

  WHO

Students from First year to Final year of Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli can participate in this event.
